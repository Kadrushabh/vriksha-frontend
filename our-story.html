Our Mission

Bringing Ayurveda to Every Home

Vriksha was born from a simple belief: that the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda, preserved for over 5,000 years, holds the key to modern wellness. In Sanskrit, "Vriksha" means tree — symbolizing growth, strength, and deep roots.

Founded in 2020, we set out to bridge the gap between traditional Ayurvedic knowledge and contemporary health needs. Every product we create honors time-tested formulations while meeting the highest standards of modern science and quality.

Our mission is to make authentic, effective Ayurvedic wellness accessible to everyone, empowering people to take charge of their health naturally.

Sustainability

Our Commitment to the Planet

At Vriksha, sustainability isn't just a buzzword — it's woven into everything we do.

  • 100% Recyclable Packaging — Our bottles are made from recycled glass and our labels from seed paper that you can plant!
  • Carbon-Neutral Shipping — We offset every delivery through verified reforestation projects.
  • Zero Waste Production — Our manufacturing facilities repurpose all byproducts into compost for partner farms.
  • Solar-Powered Facilities — Our processing units run on 100% renewable energy.
